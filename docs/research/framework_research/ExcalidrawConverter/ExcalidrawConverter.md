# Excalidraw Converter

- **URL:** https://github.com/sindrel/excalidraw-converter (~272 stars)
- **License:** MIT

CLI converting Excalidraw diagrams to Gliffy, draw.io, and Mermaid formats. Demonstrates that conversion between canvas state (JSON) and LLM-friendly text formats (Mermaid) is feasible. Our app should include similar export capabilities.
