# Penpot

- **URL:** https://github.com/penpot/penpot (~45,200 stars)
- **License:** MPL-2.0

Leading open-source Figma alternative. SVG-native output. New Rust/Skia WASM renderer (v2.x). Tile-based rendering with caching. Complete design app but ClojureScript codebase is a barrier. Best considered as architectural inspiration rather than a dependency.
