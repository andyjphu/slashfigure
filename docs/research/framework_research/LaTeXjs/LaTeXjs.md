# LaTeX.js

- **URL:** https://github.com/michael-brade/LaTeX.js (~864 stars) | **License:** MIT

Full LaTeX documents (not just math) to HTML5 via PEG.js parser. Handles `\begin{figure}`, `\begin{table}`, `\caption{}`. Parser AST could extract structured figure/table metadata. Relevant if app needs to render complete LaTeX fragments.
