# Typst

- **Full Name:** Typst
- **URL:** https://github.com/typst/typst
- **Stars:** ~52,431
- **License:** Apache-2.0
- **Language:** Rust

## What It Does
Modern markup-based typesetting system, successor to LaTeX. Compiles documents with figures, math, and diagrams. Clean syntax. Fast compilation.

## Key for Our Project
Typst's rapid growth (52K stars) signals a shift toward more LLM-friendly document formats. Its ecosystem includes CeTZ (drawing, like TikZ) and Fletcher (arrow diagrams). Could be a future export target alongside LaTeX.

## Key Insight
Typst is Rust-native, Apache-2.0, and designed for the modern era. If our app targets both LaTeX and Typst export, we cover the entire academic publishing spectrum.
